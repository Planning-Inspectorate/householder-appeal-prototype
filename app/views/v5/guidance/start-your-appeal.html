Appeal a householder planning decision

You'll need certain documents to complete your appeal:

  • the Local Planning Authority’s decision letter
  • your appeal statement

You'll also need your original planning application form, which you can search for on your Local Planning Authority's website.​

​

Your appeal statement

You’ll need to write an appeal statement that sets out why you think the Local Planning Authority's decision is incorrect. This should respond to the reasons for refusal (listed on the decision letter) and include any other information that supports your appeal.
