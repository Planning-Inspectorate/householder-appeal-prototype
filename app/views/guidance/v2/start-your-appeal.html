Start your appeal

You'll need certain documents to complete your appeal:

  • your planning application form
  • the decision letter from your local planning department
  • your appeal statement

If you do not have a copy of your planning application form, you can search for it on your local planning department's website.â€‹

You'll also need an email address. We will use this to contact you about your appeal.

Your appeal statement

As part of your appeal you will include an appeal statement. This is the only opportunity to appeal your planning decision.

How to appeal

Appealing online will speed up the appeals process.

If you cannot use the online service, contact the Planning Inspectorate customer support team on 0303 444 5000, or email: enquiries@planninginspectorate.gov.uk

You must appeal within 12 weeks of the decision date on the letter from your local planning department. If you cannot use the online service, you must leave enough time for us before the deadline to help you complete the process.
