Appeal a householder planning decision

Start your appeal

You'll need:

  • your planning application number​
  • the name of your local planning department

You'll also need your original planning application form, which you can search for on your local planning department's website.​

​

Your appeal statement

You’ll need to write an appeal statement that sets out why you think the local planning department's decision is incorrect. This should respond to all the reasons for refusal (listed on the decision letter) and include any other information that supports your appeal.

Start now

How to appeal

Applying online will speed up the appeals process.

If you cannot use the online service, contact the Planning Inspectorate customer support team on 0303 444 5000, or email:
enquiries@planninginspectorate.gov.uk
