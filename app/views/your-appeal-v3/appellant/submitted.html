What happens next?

  • The Planning Inspectorate will need to make sure your appeal is valid. This should take around 2 weeks, but may take longer in busy periods.
  • Once we’ve checked that your appeal is valid, we’ll start your appeal and let you know. 
  • Your Local council will need to submit their evidence. This usually takes about 1 week from when you submitted your appeal. We'll let you know when this happens and you can view them here.
  • A planning inspector may need to visit the property for an inspection. We will contact you to arrange this.
