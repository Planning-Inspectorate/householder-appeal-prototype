Appeal submitted

Your appeal has been submitted to the Planning Inspectorate.

We’ll send you a confirmation email and we’ll tell your local planning department about the appeal.

What happens next?

  1. Your local planning department will send you a copy of the original case file for your information only, you do not need to do anything.
  2. An Inspector may need to make a site visit. If they do, we'll be in touch to let you know:
    • if the inspector needs you to be present or not
    • when the visit will happen​
  3. The Inspector will then consider all the evidence to make their decision.

You can expect a decision on your appeal within 8 weeks.
