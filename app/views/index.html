PINS Prototypes

Introduction to Appeal a householder planning decision

The Householder Appeal Service enables a householder to have their home improvement application reconsidered. Applications are made to the Local Planning Authority (LPA), which is usually their local council and, if refused, legislation provides homeowners with a right of appeal.

These prototypes cover different journeys for the 'Appeal a householder planning decision' for users such as the appellant, agents acting on behalf of the appellant, local planning authorities, and inspectors.
