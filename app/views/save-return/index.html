Appeal a householder planning decision

You can appeal a planning decision, or search for a decision notice, on the Planning Inspectorate website.

Before you start

You'll need certain documents to complete your appeal:

  • your original planning application form
  • the Local Planning Authority’s decision notice
  • your appeal statement
Start now

Your appeal statement

You’ll need to write an appeal statement that sets out why you think the Local Planning Authority's decision is incorrect. This should respond to the reasons for refusal (listed on the LPA's decision notice) and include any other information that supports your appeal.

Guidance on writing your appeal statement.

Your appeal

You should only appeal if you think the LPA’s decision to refuse your planning application was incorrect.​

You cannot use this service to appeal a high hedges decision or a tree preservation consent order decision.

How to appeal

Applying online will speed up the appeals process.

If you cannot use the online service, contact the Planning Inspectorate customer support team on 0303 444 5000, or email:
enquiries@planninginspectorate.gov.uk
